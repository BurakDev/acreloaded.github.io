AssaultCube Reloaded (ACR)

ACR is an open-source (free) first-person-shooter. Aimed at improving AssaultCube, it combines the best traits from various popular shooting games.

It targets realistic gameplay with many settings and options to modify gameplay.

AssaultCube Reloaded, formerly AssaultCube Special Edition, is a free-and-open-source first-person-shooter, created by the AssaultCube Reloaded Task Force to improve AssaultCube.

This game combines the best of all worlds: realism from Battlefield, server-sided “anti-cheat” inspired by the Quake engine, perks from Call of Duty, mutators from Red Eclipse, and fast-paced gameplay with a low system footprint from AssaultCube and the Cube Engine.

These are just some of the numerous improvements compared to AssaultCube:

  • New, diverse game modes and mutators
  • Many new and different weapons
  • More realistic gameplay (damage fading over distance, bleeding, drowning)
  • Less potential cheats (by moving more code to the server)
  • Better voting system: admin votes don’t veto directly
  • Improved radar shows explosions and shotlines
  • Killfeed makes it easy to see how players were killed
  • Spawn enqueue/dequeue—no need to spam the spawn button
